How It Works

  1. Start Monitoring: Tap START to begin real-time audio analysis. The spectrogram immediately displays all frequencies from your microphone.
  2. Adjust Frequency Range: Drag the green handles on the spectrogram to select your target frequency band. Everything outside is filtered out instantly.
  3. Fine-Tune & Listen: Use presets for quick selection or fine-tune manually. Increase gain to amplify quiet sounds. The filtered audio plays in real-time.
  4. Record & Analyze: Tap the record button to capture raw audio. Play back recordings later and adjust filters to isolate different sounds from the same recording.
